6.snap商店添加代理
  执行：sudo systemctl edit snapd.service
  在打开的文件中加入：
    [Service]
    Environment=http_proxy=http://proxy:port
    Environment=https_proxy=http://proxy:port
  执行：sudo systemctl daemon-reload
  执行：sudo systemctl restart snapd.service
7.snap安装keepassxc
  执行：sudo snap install keepassxc
